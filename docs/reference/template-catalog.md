# Template catalog

Templates are starting points for common healthcare app ideas. You can choose a template, then narrow it during the project questions.

| Template | Use it for | Good first review |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Patient Intake Flow | Pre-visit registration, waiting-room check-in, and clinician review of submitted intake details. | Submit one intake form and confirm the clinician view shows the important details clearly. |
| Patient Vital Signs Tracker | Home monitoring, in-clinic vital entry, trends, and abnormal value follow-up. | Review one patient with normal vitals and one with an abnormal value. |
| Clinical Data Viewer | Patient summaries across demographics, conditions, allergies, medications, vitals, labs, encounters, notes, and imaging reports. | Check whether the first screen helps a clinician understand the patient quickly. |
| Clinical Decision Support Module | Medication safety checks, abnormal vital warnings, preventive care reminders, and care gap alerts. | Confirm each recommendation has a reason and can be acknowledged or dismissed. |
| PROMs Outreach | Patient-reported outcome questionnaires, completion tracking, and results review. | Complete one questionnaire and confirm the response appears for the care team. |
| Patient Engagement Workflow | Appointment preparation, adherence check-ins, symptom monitoring, education acknowledgement, and post-discharge follow-up. | Walk through one patient who completes the follow-up and one who misses it. |
| Clinical Calculator | Score calculation, risk assessment, measurement conversion, and result interpretation. | Check the inputs, calculation, result label, and interpretation text. |
| Analytics Report | Quality metrics, population monitoring, outcomes reporting, completion analysis, vital trends, and care gap drill-downs. | Confirm each metric supports a real operational or clinical decision. |

Templates are not final products. Treat them as starting points for the first useful version.
